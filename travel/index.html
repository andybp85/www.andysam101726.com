Getting there

Newark (EWR) is the nearest airport to Morristown, NJ. From the airport, you can travel to Morristown by rideshare (Uber, Lyft, etc.).

To arrive by NJ Transit train: take the Northeast Corridor line from Newark Int'l Airport to Secaucus Junction, then change at Secaucus to the Morris & Essex line to Morristown.
