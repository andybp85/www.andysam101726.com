Andy & Sam's
Wedding

What is the deadline for RSVPs?
Your RSVP is due to us by the end of day on Saturday, September 26, 2026. If you've already RSVPed and want or need to change your response, you may do so up until the deadline.
What time should I arrive?
The ceremony begins at 4:30 p.m. Please arrive with enough time to park and make your way across the hotel property to the Conservatory. The Madison Hotel has a large lot with ample parking.
Do you have a gift registry?
We do not have a registry and we are happy for you to attend our wedding regardless of any gift. If you are inclined, we are accepting contributions to our post-wedding fund through Honeyfund, which we would gratefully use toward our honeymoon and our upcoming move.
Are children invited?
To best allow everyone to enjoy a relaxing night off, our wedding is an adults-only event.
Is there a dress code?
Please come dressed to impress at whatever level of formal you prefer. Anything but a white dress is fine by us! If you'd like to "match" our event, our colors are jewel tones and the vibe is 1920s Deco/Nouveau.

(Note on shoes: high heels are safe, as the full event is indoors. The only thing happening outdoors will be some family/wedding-party photos before the ceremony, weather permitting.)
Can we take pictures and videos?
We kindly ask that all devices be stashed and silent for our ceremony, to best allow our photographer Bryan to capture the moment. At the reception, however, please take all the pictures and videos that you like — and promise to share the best ones with us after!
Does dinner include a vegetarian option?
Yes — on the day there will be an option provided for a vegetarian entrée. If you have a different dietary restriction (or a food allergy), please let us know so that we can inquire with the venue about accommodating it.
What is the venue's parking and accessibility situation?
The Madison Hotel is handicap accessible, including the parking lot and some designated hotel rooms. Our ceremony space specifically is accessible by a separate outdoor entrance. The reception space is accessible as normal from inside the rest of the hotel.
May we contact you with other questions?
Please feel free to reach out to us and/or our parents if you have other questions. You can also reply to the email from which we sent your invitation.
